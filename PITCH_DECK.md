## SLIDE 4: THE MONEY PAGES — What Converts Visitors to Clients

### 1. The Hero That Sells Without Words
The floating UI cards aren't decoration — they're **product demos**. A visitor sees a Power BI dashboard, an automation workflow, Azure uptime metrics, and analytics charts within 2 seconds of landing. Before reading a single word, they already understand what you deliver.

### 2. The Philosophy That Builds Trust
Auto-rotating accordion panels with stats (40% less complexity, 2x faster delivery, 98% retention, 99.97% uptime) — these cycle automatically, catching the eye of even casual scrollers. Each stat is backed by a description that positions you as thoughtful, not just technical.

### 3. The ROI Calculator That Captures Leads
Visitors who aren't ready to call can still engage. They input their team size, hourly cost, and hours wasted — and get a personalized savings projection with charts. **This is your 24/7 sales assistant.** It ends with "Schedule a Consultation" — the warmest CTA possible because they've already seen their own numbers.

### 4. The CTA Section That Removes Objections
Split layout: left side asks for the meeting, right side answers "what happens next" with a 3-step process. Below that: animated stats + a 5-star testimonial. Every objection is handled in one viewport.

### 5. Case Studies With Metrics
Not just "we helped Company X." Actual numbers — cost savings, time reduction, uptime improvement. Proof that backs up every claim on the site.
